About the Company

Phanerosis Furniture Enterprise is built on the belief that great furniture should combine beauty, durability and thoughtful craftsmanship. Drawing from years of industry experience, we create pieces that gives comfort and brings character into every home.

Our company specializes in the following: fitted wardrobes, bespoke kitchens, dining and living room sets, unique sofa designs, accent units, and professional reupholstery services.

At Phanerosis, quality is not an option—it is our identity.

Timeless Pieces for Today’s Living.

Knowing the Craftsman

With over two decades of hands-on experience, our lead craftsman began his journey at Sam-Seth Furniture / New Juaben Furniture in Koforidua — one of Ghana’s most respected furniture companies in the 1990s and early 2000s.


Under the guidance of seasoned experts, he mastered a wide range of furniture-making techniques, including the building of in-built wardrobes, bespoke kitchen units, premium sofa and living room sets, dining sets, TV units, and specialized reupholstery work.


Over the years, he has contributed to beautifying and furnishing over 600 homes, transforming living spaces with creativity, precision, and unmatched craftsmanship. His work blends traditional Ghanaian furniture techniques with modern design aesthetics—making each piece functional, elegant, and durable.
